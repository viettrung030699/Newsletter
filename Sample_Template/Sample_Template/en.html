TECH SOLUTIONS FOR COVID-19
Home Quarantine Tracking application - Stay Home Stay Safe
Home Quarantine Tracking application operates by monitoring body temperature and location of the quarantine in real-time. It will send alerts when these parameters exceed a threshold or when people leave the approved area.

The quarantine people will be equipped with a body temperature meter watch; the body temperature information will be synchronized with a smartphone and then the data will be transferred to the server system located in the HCM Data Center. The process is highly secure and only controlled and retrieved by authorized parties.
Eldercare application - Apply AI and IoT in monitoring the elder’s health
Eldercare solution allows users to remotely monitor their health through a combination of IoT and smartwatch.

This application will continuously observe, monitor the indicators in real-time, record and then send the data to the central server. With superior sensors, the app can measure heart rate, heart rate variability, blood pressure, SpO2, electrocardiogram and alert if any abnormal signs occur in the body.

AI technology is also incorporated to give users/ doctors recommendations for the most appropriate treatments or interventions.
Clinic Kit Box - Smart multi-purpose healthcare box
Clinic Kit is a multi-purpose box that supports collecting healthcare indicators in real-time and allows patients to connect to doctors whenever they need, either in hospitals, homes, or other places. Based on the users’ data, doctors can follow and understand the patient's health status.

The application can monitor all of the patient's health indexes, including heart rate, heart rate variability, blood pressure, saturation of peripheral oxygen, etc. Besides, it helps store, analyze, and export relevant data for future diagnosis to provide the doctor if there is a request to check the patient’s health in the past. Moreover, users and doctors can make direct video calls, especially in the emergency, the platform supports patients with an SOS button.
